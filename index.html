About This Site

just4fun is a Spider-Man fan zone built with pure HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no fuss. It converts the raw energy of Spider-Man video clips into stunning web animations powered by the Canvas API and CSS keyframes.

Drop your favourite Spider-Man clips into the assets/ folder, uncomment the <video> tag in index.html, and watch the hero section come alive with your own footage. Check assets/README.md for full instructions.

  • 🎨 Spider-Verse glitch & chromatic-aberration CSS effects
  • 🕸️ Canvas cursor web-trail (requestAnimationFrame)
  • 📜 Intersection Observer scroll-reveal animations
  • 💥 Click burst & Thwip! web-shooting interactions
  • 📱 Fully responsive — mobile, tablet, desktop
